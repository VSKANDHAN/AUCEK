UCEK LIBRARY

UCEK Library was established in 2010.The library has 5274 Volumes of books including reference books and Bibiliograpy.We provide book database and internet browsing for its users. Library working hours is from 9.00 am to 04.40 pm on all Week days.Books will be issued in the working hours.

MEMBERSHIP DETAILS
  • Books will be issued only on production of valid ID card.
  • Books borrowed must be returned on or before the due date.
  • Renewal of the books cannot the more than one time.
  • Books will have to be physically presented for renewals.
  • Books will be renewed if there are no reservations.
  • If the books are missed by the user, he/she will replace the books at any cost.
  • Users are requested to verify the physical condition of the book/s before borrowing.
  • Users are requested to place their bags/belongings outside the library.
  • Users are requested to Maintain absolute Silence in the library.
  • Users are required to keep the library and the campus neat and tidy.
