ELECTRICAL & ELECTRONICS ENGINEERING

The Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering of University College of Engineering Kancheepuram was established in the year 2010. The department offers B.E Fulltime Programme in Electrical and Electronics Engineering. The intake for undergraduate programme is 60. The department has fully equipped laboratories.

There is One Assistant Professor in the department and 6 Teaching Fellows. The faculty members are specialized in the areas of Power Electronics, Power systems, Embedded Systems and Mechatronics. The goal of the department is to motivate the students towards higher studies and to promote research in our Institution thereby providing value added education to the student community.

Mission

To pursue our vision by striving for excellence in creating, applying, and imparting knowledge in Computer Science and Engineering.To pursue comprehensive educational system, research in collaboration with industry and government and to disseminate knowledge through scholarly publications.To provide service through professional societies to the community, the state, and the nation.

Vission

To uniquely position the department and to establish synergistic relationship across the entire spectrum of disciplines involved with computing by our faculty contributing to Computer Science and devoting themselves to take the maximal advantage of modern Computer Science to solve a wide range of complex, scientific, technological and social problems.
