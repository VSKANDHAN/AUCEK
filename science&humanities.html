SCIENCE AND HUMANITIES

The Department of Science and Humanities was established in the year 2010. The Department of Science and Humanities with the faculty strength of 11 members from English, Chemistry, Physics and Maths. The strength of the department is the young faculty members who really work hard for the establishment of the department.

The faculty members are motivated to carry out research activities in the frontier areas of science and technology and most of them are pursing towards the Ph.D research programmes in their disciplines.
